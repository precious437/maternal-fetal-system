Help & User Guides

Step-by-step guides to help you use the Maternal-Fetal AI Surgical Prediction System effectively

🚀

Getting Started

First time setup and basic navigation

1. Log in using your credentials.

2. Complete your surgeon profile in Settings.

3. Go to Surgical AI page to start a new case.

4. Begin by entering vital signs, then upload images for YOLO analysis.

🧠

Using Surgical AI Features

Vital signs, YOLO detection, and predictions

Vital Signs: Use only numeric values. All fields are required before running prediction.

YOLO Detection: Upload ultrasound or MRI image. The system will automatically detect fetal head, placenta, and possible anomalies.

AI Prediction: Click "Run Full AI Prediction" after entering vitals and uploading image. Results include risk score and clinical recommendation.

Live Vision: Use your camera for real-time detection during scanning.

🕹️

Live Vision & 3D Rehearsal

Real-time assistance and surgical practice

Live Vision Mode: Click "Start Live Camera" and point your device at the ultrasound screen or patient area. YOLO will detect structures in real time.

3D Rehearsal Mode: Switch to this mode to practice surgical procedures. Connect your PS joystick for realistic tool control.

Use Live Vision during actual scanning and 3D Rehearsal before high-risk deliveries.
